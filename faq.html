    IS IT A SEPARATE GAME FROM MINECRAFT OR OTHER GAMES?

    As strange as it sounds, we are moving away from Minecraft this time to develop a completely new and separate game, built on a graphics engine. So yes, you don't depend on Minecraft, or other platforms.

    CAN I PLAY WITH MY FRIENDS?

    Sure. Although you require an available connection on your device. The game will require an internet connection to access.

    HOW LONG IS THE AVERAGE DURATION IN STORY MODE IN THE GAM?

    The average duration of the game is not confirmed, due to its development. However, we are considering about 6 hours of gameplay. The hours of games with your friends, though, will be to your liking.

    WHAT PLATFORMS WILL THE GAME BE RELEASED FOR?

    We're still discussing that, but it's coming out on Itch.io, and possibly the WCord Store and Steam. It will be difficult, but we will try.

    WHICH OPERATING SYSTEMS WILL BE OPERATIONAL?

    In idea, Windows (64-bit version) only. It is the most stable operating system for video games, and the game contains innovative elements of the industry. Such as Dynamic Lights, Nvidia DLSS and AMD FSR for screen resolution. Of course, your computer will require at least DirectX11.

    Will my computer be compatible with the game?

    For weeks, we have been analysing our users' data with WCoreMC, to gain some insight into the hardware. And we are in optimisation, to make it as playable as possible. Try WCoreMC
