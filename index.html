    WHAT IS THE UNIVERSE DEFENSOR?

    The Universe Defensor is a project still in development. This project was born among rumors in 2021 of a video game developed separately from other platforms or services. This game seeks strategy in solo or cooperative mode with your friends. It is based on automatic survival on an island, with the resources your allies find, you can build defenses against enemy attacks.
