    WHAT IS WCORD GAME PROTOTYPE?

    The WCord Game Prototype is a project still without an official name. This project was born among rumors in 2021 of a video game developed separately from other platforms or services. This game seeks strategy in solo or cooperative mode with your friends. It is based on automatic survival on an island, with the resources your allies find, you can build defenses against enemy attacks.
